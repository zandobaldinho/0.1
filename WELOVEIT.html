WE LOVE IT

A dark and imposing building stands, completely black, with yellow windows that simulate lights on inside. At the entrance, two skeletal figures are positioned as if they are guardians of the door, creating an atmosphere of mystery and a sense of horror. The sky in the background is a deep blue, contrasting with the yellow light from the windows. The scene conveys a feeling of abandonment and unease, with the skeletal figures adding a macabre touch to the setting.
