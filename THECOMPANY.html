THE COMPANY PT.2

An imposing and dark company building stands, entirely black, with yellow windows that mimic lights on inside, creating a vibrant contrast with the surrounding darkness. In the background, a large yellow moon shines brightly, illuminating the dark blue sky of the night, while bats fly silently in front of the moon, their silhouettes cast in the lunar glow. A gothic tree, with twisted branches and dark leaves, stands out against the moonlight, enhancing the mystery of the scene. At the entrance of the building, two skeletal figures are positioned as if they are sinister guardians, observing the surroundings with a ghostly presence. On the roof of the company, a person sits calmly, smoking, creating a surreal and unsettling scene. The atmosphere is heavy with mystery, evoking a sense of abandonment and strangeness, where darkness and light intertwine in a dark and captivating way.
